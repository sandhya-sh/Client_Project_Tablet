UMSI Peer Tutoring Program

In partnership with our UMSI faculty, the UMSI Peer Tutoring program is an opportunity for UMSI students and students taking UMSI courses to engage with peer tutors and receive assistance and support in UMSI courses. For more information regarding student support and tutoring, please email umsi.academicsuccess@umich.edu

Frequently Asked Questions

1. How do I request a tutor?

Fill out our Tutor Request Form or email umsi.academicsuccess@umich.edu with your course details and availability.

2. Are online sessions available?

Yes! We offer both in-person and virtual appointments. When scheduling, just note your preference, and we’ll send you a Zoom link if needed.

3. Do I have to pay for tutoring?

Tutoring is free for all UMSI students. Our goal is to make academic support accessible to everyone.
